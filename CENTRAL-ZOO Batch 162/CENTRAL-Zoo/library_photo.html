Wild animal.

Wildlife traditionally refers to undomesticated animal species, but has come to include all organisms that grow or live wild in an area without being introduced by humans.[1] Wildlife can be found in all ecosystems. Deserts, forests, rainforests, plains, grasslands, and other areas, including the most developed urban areas, all have distinct forms of wildlife.

Interesting things about animals

Shark

A shark can have up to 30,000 teeth in its lifetime. The ocean's top predator has many rows of teeth below the visible row of teeth.

Starling

There are many breeds of ornamental birds that can be trained to speak exactly the same way as a human. If your life is too dull, then this is definitely the best pet for you.

Cheetah

Cheetah is the fastest species in the world, with a running speed that can reach 112-120 km / h .They can reach a top speed in 60 seconds .

Jellyfish

The longest is a giant jellyfish species 75 m (equal to 6 buses in tandem), then whales with a length of 18m, crocodile, python, stingray up to 8 m in length.
